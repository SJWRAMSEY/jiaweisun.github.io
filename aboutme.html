About Me

Nick Chimitt (PhD, Year 1) received a B.Sc. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering in 2017 at Purdue University Northwest. He is currently a PhD student in the school of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University, West Lafayette, IN. His research interests include modeling and mitigating atmospheric turbulence in images, machine learning, image restoration, and inverse problems. He also has experience with communications, adaptive filtering, and numerical optimization.
